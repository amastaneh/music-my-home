**Style**

Language: Persian (All parenthesized lines are phonetic transliterations for reference only and should not be performed.)

Atmosphere: Dark, mournful, and tense, gradually opening toward dignity, resistance, and resolve.

Genre: Epic-Tragic, Cinematic Orchestral, Fast Dark Pop-Rap crossover.

Vibe: Deeply emotional with strong dynamic contrast, fragile lament → fast, rhythmic, staccato intensity → explosive, cracked-voice climax.

Vocal Arrangement:

- Opening Lament: Dual vocals (female lead + male support). Female voice carries the emotional cry; male voice provides low, restrained harmony. Raw and mournful.

- Chorus (“ایرانم… / مادرم ایرانم…”): Dual vocals (male + female). Long, sustained delivery. Anthemic, powerful, and emotionally expansive—the emotional peak of the piece.
