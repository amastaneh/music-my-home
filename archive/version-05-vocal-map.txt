**Style**

Persian Voices

Atmosphere:

Dark, mournful, and tense — gradually opening toward dignity, resistance, and resolve


Vocal Arrangement:

Opening Lament Section: Dual vocals (male + female) layered together. Female voice carries the cry; male voice supports with low, restrained harmony. Emotional, raw, and mournful.

Climactic Chorus (“ایرانم… / مادرم ایرانم…”): Dual vocals (male + female). Long, sustained, stretched delivery. Anthemic, powerful, and emotionally expansive — the emotional peak of the piece.

Genre: Epic-Tragic, Cinematic Orchestral, Dark Pop-Rap crossover.

Vibe: Deeply emotional and mournful. High dynamic contrast: Starts with slow, soft weeping vocals -> transitions into a fast, rhythmic, staccato, intense vocal delivery -> culminates in explosive, heart-wrenching screams (cracked voice).
